Jack and Landlords

Landlords' Business Smart Way to Lease

Jack and Landlords is a capital partner with landlords of multi-family and single family property management companies.
Our innovative way of partnering with landlords in leasing to tenants, as an alternative to security deposits surplus line coverage, generates perpetual revenue, retention, and growth.

Retention

After your guarantee is paid in full the first year, your tenants will see a reduction in their second year rent even with a 4% rent increase. This gives tenants an incentive to renew a second year and potentially a third year.

Profit Tax Free

Your guarantee pays out the entire amount equal to your tenants' 1st month's rent for any tenant damages and default rent payments, thereby, replacing the security deposit. When damages are lower than your guarantee payout, then the profit is tax free to your company.

About Us

Jack and Landlords is underwritten by Selective Insurance Group and founded in 2014 out of Charlotte, NC with the mission of mutually benefiting landlords, tenants, and owners with no security deposit leasing.
